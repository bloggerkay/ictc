HERE ARE SOME RULES OF THE ICT CENTER

Do not change PC settings

Keep password a secret

Do not open unapproved sites

Listen and follow instructions

Do not bring drink or food near the Keyboard

Do not erase anything from the PC

Do not connect or Disconnect any cable

Do not plug a USB or any other device into the PC

Do not install personal softwares on any PC

Keep sound level to a minimum to avoid disturbance

Avoid unneccessary noice at the Lab

Re-arrange chairs and PC components as they were after usage
